Mental Health is relevant to many people and sometimes getting help can be difficult. It can impact our actions, growth, and minds.

Mental Health... What is it?

Mental Health can be someones emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It can impact us throughout our whole lives, so taking care of it is an big responsibility since it can affect our choices, thinking, feelings and actions.

Why we need to talk about it

Without taking care of your mental health it can cause a lot of damage to someone. For example, they may not have the ability to do everyday tasks, develop a mental illness or disorder, not feeling the best, and much more. Sometimes mental health isn’t accessible to some because of the stigma, misconceptions about it, and don’t know who to trust or get help from.
