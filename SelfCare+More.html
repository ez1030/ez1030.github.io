Self Care

  1. Exercise - This can help with your mood and health.
  2. Getting enough sleep - Having a good sleeping schedule so you have enough energy, good mood, and brain performance. Also reducing screen time because of blue light.
  3. Relaxting Activities and Hobbies - Do something you like or try something new. Try healthy activities, maybe you like journaling or drawing.
  4. Have goals and priorties - You will know what is important in your life and what can wait.

Self-Care looks different for everyone so it is important to find the things you love. These are just some examples. Make sure to do things that are good for you, something that clears your mind and relaxing.
